CHUNG HOM KOK BEACH

Chung Hom Kok beach is situated in away from the hustle and bustle of the nearby beaches such as Stanley beach, deep water bay beach and repulse bay beach, and quite difficult to find - you will have to keep your eyes opened for several flights of staris that will take you down to this isolated beach, since it is hidden by the lush green that surrounds it. Therefore, on this tiny beach that overlooks the western shore of Stanley, you can peacefully enjoy the fresh smell and sound of the ocean while relaxing on the soft sand, or you can sit down and have a delicious barbeque at the free grills, while watching as the sun sets beyond the vast sea.

How to get there:

  1. Take bus 6, 66, or 6X from Exchange square Central
  2. Get off at Chung Hom Kok station and walk to down the steps to the beach
