SHEK O BEACH

Another one of the beaches in Hong Kong that is underappreciated due to its detachment from the center of Hong Kong, Shek O beach is no longer than a 45 minute bus ride that is away from Shau kei wan MTR station. On this beachside village, you can grab food from any nearby store (that includes cha chan tengs, Thai food, fruit stores, and even breakfast food) and make your way to the beach, which is completely safe as it is watched over by lifeguards. You may even rent out beach equipment to heighten your time spent in the crystalline waters of Shek O beach.

How to get there:

  1. Take MTR to Shau Kei Wan station
  2. Exit from exit A
  3. Take mini bus 9 from bus terminus
  4. Get off at the last stop and walk to the beach (you should be able to see it right away. If not, follow the crowd!)
