Lifetastic

Situated in the western, less populated side of Hong Kong, Lifetastic is a beautifully designed restaurant that comes with new decorations per season or holiday to fit the mood. Visited most for their strawberry watermelon cake, they also serve many other delicious dishes including a range of pastas, drinks, and desserts. While having a meal, you may even relax on the sofa for a chat with friends - With such mesmerising surroundings and mouth-watering food, once you step in, you'll never want to leave!

How to get there:

  1. Take the MTR to HKU station
  2. Exit from exit D and walk up a small hill to the restaurant
