RABBITLAND CAFE

The first ever rabbit cafe in Hong Kong, Rabbitland cafe, is situated on the third floor of a building on Jaffe road, Causeway bay. It may be difficult to find, but once you arrive, you are greeted by a a dozen bunnies awaiting your arrival behind a baby door. Not only does this cafe satiate your sweet tooth, you can also spend an hour playing with and feeding the rabbits their favorite treats (you are provided with alchohol hand sanitiser to prevent harming either yourself or the rabbits). Before you go, make sure you reserve seats, otherwise you may be battling with other customers to feed your favorite rabbit!

How to get there:

3/F No 530 Jaffe Road Causeway Bay
