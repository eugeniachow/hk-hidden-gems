PEDAL BOATING

Pedal boating at wong chuk hang reservoir park is the ideal way to spend your afternoon away from the bustling cityside, and into the serene natural side of Hong Kong. For 80-120 dollars (depending on boat size and time), you can enjoy a peaceful ride on the green waters, while being able to appreciate the habitats of the sea animals living beneath your boat - including turtles! The pedal boats all provide shade, so you can enjoy this activity even during the summer.

How to get there:

  1. Take bus 6 or 66 from C entralexchange square bus terminus/bus 5 or 76 from Causeway Bay/bus 41A or 63 from North Point
  2. Alight at Wong Nai Chung Reservoir Park bus stop (near the petrol station)
