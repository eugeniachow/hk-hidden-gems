Whitehead BBQ

This barbecue hotspot may sound far away, but is defintely worth the distance traveled to get there. It is the perfect location to appreciate Hong Kong's local food through cooking your own with friends and family. It is also decorated with golden lights that fill up the whole area, making it a beautiful place not only to have a meal, but to spend time with loved ones while watching the sunset beside you!

How to get there:

  1. Take the MTR to Wu Kai Sha station
  2. Walk to Whitehead, Pak Shek Kok
