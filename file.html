INSPIRATION LAKE

Inspiration lake is a beautiful lake located besides the wonderland of Disney. It is a gorgeous lake surrounded by greenery and fountains, and you can even catch yourself some turtles by the shore! So grab out your wallets, rent a pedal boat and go pedalling around the lake to enjoy your afternoon. You can also grab a bit at the convenience store (the fanciest 7 11 by far!), and sit down on the grass and enjoy a picnic!
