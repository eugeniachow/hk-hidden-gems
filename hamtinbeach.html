HAM TIN BEACH

Only a thirty minute speedboat ride away from Sai Kung ferry pier, Ham Tin beach is known to be one of the cleanest beaches in Hong Kong! Secluded as it is, the water is crystal clear, and the sand is still visible when you're past waist-deep into the water. There is no better place for you to freely enjoy the summer sun without being bothered by tourists, and this experience is comparable to a day in the Maldives.

How to get there:

  1. Take MTR to Choi Hung station
  2. Exit from exit A2
  3. Take mini bus 1A or bus 92 from bus terminus
  4. Get off at the last stop and take any of the speed boats by the ferry pier that takes you to Ham Tin beach (cost will be around $100-$200)
