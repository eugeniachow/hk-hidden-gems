AH MEOW CAT CAFE

The perfect getaway for any Hong Konger who wants a break from the ubiquitous clattering cha chan tengs, Ah meow cat cafe welcomes anyone on the third floor of Po ming building, facing lee theatres to enter for a meal or a snack (some food even has imprints of cats on them too!). When setting foot into Ah meow cafe, be sure not to step on any of the eight cats awaiting your arrival. These cats all have their names posted on the board near the entrance, and there you can familiarize yourself with these cafe pets, and you may even be lucky enough to have a seat next to a few kittens!

How to get there:

Flat D-E, 3/F Po Ming Building, Fu Ming Street, Causeway Bay, Hong Kong
