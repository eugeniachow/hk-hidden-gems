Victoria Peak garden

When most people think of the peak, they think of the splendid views that can be seen from the sky terrace or the peak tower, but up the hill via mount austin road, there is a less crowded spot to watch the sunset, or simply to have a picnic. If you aren't a fan of sittin on grass, the park has plenty of benches that are intricately designed to sit on, and while you're here, you may even see several engaged couples having their wedding photos taken!

How to get there:

  1. From the peak galleria, turn on to mount Austin road and walk uphill until you see the garden
