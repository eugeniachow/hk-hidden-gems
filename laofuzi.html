LAO FU ZI RESTAURANT

This lao fu zi restaurant is a creative hub for those looking for a declicious meal or snack to eat, and more importantly, the fans of the well-known lao fu zi comics, which were enjoyed by Hong Kongers since 1962, when they were often found in newspapers and magazines. This comic is still in publication today and is widely popular, and this restaurant was inspired by the characters and punchlines of the Chinese "manhua". This restaurant's interior is decorated by images of the famous characters of the comic, and the dishes on the menu spark memories to those who have read the comics previously, as they refer back to the storylines. The lao fu zi restarurant is a bright place, and is a perfect place for people of all ages to enjoy a meal at any time of the day!

How to get there:

777 Nathan Road
